## 3. Google Reviews

[Google Reviews](https://headsonpillows.com/top-15-best-hotel-review-websites-for-hotels/) stands as one of the most trusted platforms for hotel reviews due to its massive user base and integration with Google Maps and Search. The platform lets travelers read and write reviews directly through Google Search or Maps, making it incredibly accessible for both casual and frequent travelers.

Google Reviews brings authenticity to hotel reviews since most reviewers must have a Google account, which helps reduce fake reviews. The platform displays ratings on a 5-star scale and shows review counts prominently in search results.

**Pricing:** Free for both travelers reading reviews and hotels receiving reviews. Hotels can respond to reviews and manage their business profile at no cost.

**Key features:**

- Direct integration with Google Maps for easy location-based searching
- Photos from both guests and property owners
- Verified reviews from actual Google users
- Real-time review notifications for business owners
- Quick access to room rates and booking options
- Easy sorting and filtering of reviews by rating and date
- Mobile-friendly interface across all devices
- Business response capability for hotels

## 4. Booking.com

[Booking.com](https://www.booking.com/) stands as one of the largest hotel review and booking platforms in the world. The site combines verified guest reviews with an extensive database of accommodations, making it a trusted resource for travelers looking to make informed decisions about their stays.

The platform displays ratings based on actual stays and requires guests to have a confirmed reservation to leave a review. This system helps maintain review authenticity and provides reliable feedback about hotel experiences.

Each property listing includes detailed information about amenities, room types, and location. The site also shows clear photos and highlights both positive and negative aspects of each property through guest feedback.

**Pricing:** Free to use for travelers. Hotels pay a commission on bookings made through the platform, typically ranging from 15-25% of the reservation cost.

**Key features:**

- Verified guest reviews from actual stays
- Price comparison tool for different dates and room types
- Real-time availability updates
- Detailed property descriptions and photos
- Filter options for amenities, location, and price
- Mobile app for easy booking on the go
- Loyalty program with special discounts
- Multiple language support
- 24/7 customer service
- Instant booking confirmation
